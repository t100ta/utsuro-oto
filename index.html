UtsuroOto（空ろ音）

Play Reachy Mini like a theremin — your hand controls pitch and volume in mid-air, no instrument needed.

Features

🖐️ Hand Control

Move your hand left/right to change pitch (left = high, right = low — theremin convention, mirrored from camera).
Move it up/down to control volume (top = loud, bottom = silent).
Remove your hand for >1.5 s → silence; robot head returns to neutral.

🎵 Scales & Instruments

6 scales: pentatonic major/minor, natural major/minor, blues, chromatic.
8 GM timbres active by default — flute, violin, choir aahs, trumpet, oboe, warm pad, synth voice, shakuhachi.
All 128 General MIDI instruments available. MIDI range C3–C6.

⚙️ Live Dashboard

Built-in web dashboard (port 8042) shows the live camera feed, current note name, and lets you switch scale and instrument on the fly.
Settings persist to config.json across restarts.

🎼 How to Play

1

Stand in front of Reachy Mini

Position yourself so the robot's camera can clearly see your hand. Good lighting helps MediaPipe detect your palm reliably.

2

Raise one hand and move left/right for pitch

Sweep right-to-left for low-to-high pitch (mirrored like a real theremin). Notes snap to the active scale automatically — no out-of-tune notes.

3

Move up/down to control volume

Top of frame = loudest, bottom = silent. Hold steady on a note to sustain it; move smoothly to glide between notes. The robot head follows your hand.

Install on Your Reachy Mini

UtsuroOto runs as a reachy_mini_apps plugin on the robot — not in the browser. Install it once and launch it any time from the Reachy Mini dashboard.

📦 Install via pip

Run the following command on your Reachy Mini (or in your development environment):

pip install git+https://github.com/pollen-robotics/utsuro-oto.git

After installation, restart the Reachy Mini dashboard — UtsuroOto will appear as a launchable app.
Entry point registered as: utsuro_oto.main:UtsuroOto
